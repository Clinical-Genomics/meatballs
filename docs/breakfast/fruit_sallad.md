# Fruit salad

![fruit_salad](../img/pasta-salad-horizontal-jpg-1522265695.jpeg)

## Ingredients

Leftover fruits from the CG fruit baskets

- banana
- oranges
- apples
- kiwis

## Preparations

Slice and dice the fruit and pour it into a bowl.
