# Toblerone Mousse
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/64133676/142201816-08c6eb50-f24e-4746-8bce-cf1d22fa48cc.png)

## Ingredients:
- Toblerone or other chocolate
- Cream
- Eggs

## Preparation:
1. melt the chocolate
2. add the eggs
3. whip the cream and add it to the chocolate

**Enjoy!!!**
